About Ahsan Ali

I am a student at CUNY City Tech, and my major is Computer Systems.

I chose to make this website because I want to share my passion in photography.

I really liked the UI and wanted to replicate something similar.
Here you can not only view a portion of photos I've taken through the years,
you can also view the tools and equipment I use. Please note, this is a hobby of mine and by no means am I a professional.
